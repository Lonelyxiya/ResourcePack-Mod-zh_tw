Hammerhead Sharks are large, cartelegous fish well known for their predatory habits, that live in warmer waters. Hammerheads will target any creature
 that is beneath half health, being scavangers. They also love tropical fish and squids. When seeking out food, Hammerhead
 Sharks circle their prey beforehand, to scope out an ideal attack angle. Occassionally, when attacking prey, the Hammerhead
 may loose one of its many teeth. These teeth can be used to create arrows that can travel better underwater and deal extra
 damage to aquatic creatures.
